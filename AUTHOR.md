# Author

Recursant is built by **Anders Jensen-Waud**.

I'm an engineer and product person working at the intersection of enterprise
software, AI, and governance. Recursant grew out of years of watching
organisations try to deploy AI agents into regulated environments — and
running into the same wall every time: there was no equivalent of a service
mesh for agents. No way to enforce policy across vendors. No audit trail
that satisfied a security review. No runtime guardrail enforcement that
worked across LangChain, AgentForce, Databricks, and a homegrown Python
agent at once.

I'm open-sourcing Recursant because I think the problem is bigger than any
one company should solve alone. The agentic shift is happening; the
governance layer for it should be a shared, scrutinable piece of
infrastructure — not a closed-source black box per vendor.
